AI ROI Visibility Challenge

One of the most compelling market drivers is the inability of enterprises to confidently evaluate their AI return on investment. With only 51% of enterprises able to assess AI ROI, there is a massive opportunity for platforms that provide cost transparency, performance tracking, and value attribution capabilities.

Market Insight: The gap between AI spending growth (36% increase in monthly budgets) and ROI visibility (only 51% can evaluate returns) creates an urgent need for observability platforms that connect technical metrics to business outcomes. This positions AI observability not just as a technical tool but as a business intelligence platform for AI investments.

This challenge is exacerbated by the rapid growth in high-spend AI implementations, with enterprises spending over $100K per month more than doubling from 20% to 45% year-over-year. As AI budgets scale, the pressure to demonstrate clear ROI intensifies proportionally.
