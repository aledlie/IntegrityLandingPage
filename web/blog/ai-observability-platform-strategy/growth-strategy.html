Hybrid PLG + Sales Model

The most successful SaaS companies in 2025 combine product-led growth for efficient acquisition with sales-assisted expansion for high-value accounts—a hybrid model that maximizes both volume and deal size.

"The most resilient SaaS companies in 2025 won't be purely PLG or SLG; they'll be 'Product-Led Sales Assisted.' They use PLG to efficiently acquire and qualify users at scale, then strategically deploy sales resources to convert high-potential accounts and drive enterprise expansion."

— Stage2 Capital, PLG Playbook for B2B Startups

This validates Integrity Studio's phased go-to-market strategy combining PLG foundation (Phase 1) with enterprise sales acceleration (Phase 2+), representing industry best practice for platforms targeting both SMB and enterprise segments.
