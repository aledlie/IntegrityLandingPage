## Legal Basis for Appointment

This appointment is made in accordance with:

- **GDPR Article 37**: Designation of the data protection officer
- **GDPR Article 38**: Position of the data protection officer
- **GDPR Article 39**: Tasks of the data protection officer

### Appointment Rationale

Integrity Studio LLC voluntarily designates a Data Protection Officer to:

1. Demonstrate commitment to data protection best practices
2. Provide a dedicated point of contact for data subjects and supervisory authorities
3. Ensure ongoing compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations
4. Support client trust in our AI consulting and observability services

---

## DPO Responsibilities

Per GDPR Article 39, the Data Protection Officer shall:

### 1. Inform and Advise
- Inform and advise the organization and employees of their obligations under GDPR and other data protection laws
- Provide guidance on data protection impact assessments (DPIAs)
- Advise on privacy by design and default principles

### 2. Monitor Compliance
- Monitor compliance with GDPR, other applicable data protection laws, and internal policies
- Oversee data protection training and awareness programs
- Conduct internal audits and assessments

### 3. Cooperate with Supervisory Authorities
- Act as the contact point for supervisory authorities on issues relating to processing
- Consult with supervisory authorities on data protection matters as needed
- Facilitate communication during investigations or inquiries

### 4. Handle Data Subject Requests
- Serve as the contact point for data subjects exercising their rights
- Oversee the process for responding to access, rectification, erasure, and other requests
- Ensure timely response within regulatory timeframes

### 5. Risk Management
- Conduct and oversee data protection impact assessments for high-risk processing
- Maintain records of processing activities (ROPA)
- Monitor and report on data protection risks

---

## Independence and Resources

### Independence (Article 38)

The Data Protection Officer:

- Shall not receive instructions regarding the exercise of DPO tasks
- Shall not be dismissed or penalized for performing DPO tasks
- Reports directly to the highest management level
- Has authority to raise data protection concerns to executive leadership

### Resources

The organization shall provide:

- Sufficient time to fulfill DPO duties
- Access to personal data and processing operations
- Resources to maintain expert knowledge
- Access to legal counsel when needed
- Budget for training and professional development

### Confidentiality

The Data Protection Officer is bound by confidentiality concerning the performance of their tasks, in accordance with applicable law.
